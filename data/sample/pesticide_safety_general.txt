Title: General Pesticide Safety Guidelines

Category: Safety

Before use:
- Read the entire product label and SDS.
- Confirm crop, pest, and registration status in your country.
- Calibrate sprayer and use correct PPE.

During use:
- Do not eat, drink, or smoke while handling pesticides.
- Avoid spraying in strong wind or near bee activity.
- Never guess dosage; use only label-approved rates.

If information is missing:
- Do not invent mixing instructions or dosage.
- Consult a licensed agricultural advisor or the product manufacturer.
- Check the physical product label on the container.

After use:
- Triple-rinse containers and dispose according to local regulations.
- Wash PPE separately from household laundry.
