Product: AgroShield 720 SC (example)
Active ingredient: Chlorothalonil 720 g/L
Type: Fungicide

Registered crops: Tomato, potato, cucurbits (check local label)

Target: Early blight, late blight, anthracnose (label-dependent)

Application (example label guidance):
- Tomato early blight: 1.5 L per hectare in 200-400 L water.
- Interval: 7-10 days; maximum 4 applications per season unless label states otherwise.

Safety:
- Harmful if swallowed. Avoid drift to water bodies.
- REI (re-entry): follow label (often 12-24 hours).
- Store locked away from food and feed.

Note: Always use the official product label for your country and registration.
