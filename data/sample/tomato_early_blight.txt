Title: Tomato Early Blight (Alternaria solani)

Crop: Tomato
Category: Disease management

Symptoms:
Brown concentric rings on older leaves, yellowing around spots, leaf drop in severe cases.

Treatment:
- Apply a fungicide containing chlorothalonil every 7-10 days when conditions are humid.
- Remove and destroy infected leaves; do not compost them.
- Avoid overhead irrigation; water at the base of plants.
- Improve spacing for air circulation.

Safety:
Always read the product label before application. Wear gloves and eye protection.
