Product: WheatGuard Pro 250 EC (example)
Active ingredient: Propiconazole 250 g/L
Type: Fungicide (triazole)

Registered crops: Wheat, barley (check local label)

Target: Yellow rust, brown rust, powdery mildew (label-dependent)

Application (example label guidance):
- Wheat yellow rust: 0.5 L per hectare at first sign of disease.
- Do not apply within 35 days of harvest unless label allows.

Safety:
- Wear chemical-resistant gloves and mask during mixing and spraying.
- Do not tank-mix with products unless label permits.
- Toxic to aquatic organisms; buffer zones required near water.

Note: Dosage and intervals must match the registered label for your region.
