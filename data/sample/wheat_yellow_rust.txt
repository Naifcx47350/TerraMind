Title: Wheat Yellow Rust (Puccinia striiformis)

Crop: Wheat
Category: Disease management

Symptoms:
Bright yellow stripes on leaves, reduced photosynthesis, yield loss if untreated early.

Treatment:
- Spray propiconazole or tebuconazole at early infection stages.
- Use locally approved resistant varieties where available.
- Monitor fields weekly during cool, wet spring weather.

Safety:
Follow label rates and pre-harvest intervals. Do not exceed maximum applications per season.
